Our mission


Lingua International was created by students for students. We combine all our knowledge to make it as easy, cheap and quick as possible for you to apply to hundreds of university programs and language courses in the UK.

How We Work


To apply with us is 100% free! We will never ask you for money. Thanks to our arrangements with universities and language schools, you can save up to 30% of the programme or course total cost. Applying is as simple as registering and uploading the required document. You can do it from your phone or pick up where you left off. Once the application is submitted you will get a response within 24h.

Why Us


As previous students ourselves, we know how daunting the application process can be. That's why we do all the paperwork for you, so you don't have to worry about a thing. You can reach us 24/7. We speak English, Russian, Polish, Italian, German and Ukrainian.

We take security seriously


Your personal details are always encrypted and never shared with third parties. We are a fully regulated business registered in the UK.
