How to apply

Easy

Great news! It has never been easier to apply to UK Universities.

Cut the cost

Register with us and save up to 30% of the total cost of the programme, compared to applying directly to the university.

Secure

We don’t take payments from you. We are 100% free and secure.

Search your favourite programme

Browse our catalogue for courses and programmes in the best UK universities and language schools.

Register to out student dashboard.

Registering is super easy. Once you have found the courses or programme which you like you can register here

Create your student profile

Upload the required documents. University and Language schools require different documents but don’t worry, you can find detailed instruction in your dashboard. If in doubt, you can contact us anytime. No need to provide everything at once. Update your profile and come back to it when you prefer. Once you are ready, submit your application, sit back and relax.

Processing...

We will process your application within 24h. We will let you know if you’re application was complete or if you need to provide more info.

Getting Ready

You will receive an online interview invitation. A personal advisor will prepare you to the interview. Now it is also a good time to prepare your bank statement which will be required by the university before the final offer. Pay your fees directly to the university. Remember to do this before the deadline and get a discount through Lingua International.

Pack up and start your adventure…

Don't forget to take the most important with you...
